Bröd & Salt Regeringsgatan

Bröd & Salt is a local chain of artisan bakeries, with locations all over Stockholm. Everything is fresh, made from sourdough, and lactose-free. Besides fresh bread, buns, and cookies Bröd & Salt also has a selection of fresh salads, sandwiches, and wraps if you’re hungry for lunch!

Address:

Regeringsgatan 48

Learn more

Fabrique Klarabergsgatan

Fabrique is a local chain of traditional wood-fired bakeries that are located throughout central Stockholm. Here you'll find top-quality fresh-baked sourdough bread, rolls, and cakes baked by hand with natural ingredients according to time-honored traditions. Don´t miss out on the famous blueberry buns!

Address:

Klarabergsgatan 60

Learn more

Café Pascal Vasastan

A block from busy Odenplan, on a quiet corner, you'll find Café Pascal. In 2019 the coffee house won the prestigious Gulddraken award for best café in Stockholm. Great coffee and sandwiches with shrimp or porchetta to die for!

Address:

Norrtullsgatan 4
